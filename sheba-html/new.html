Sheba is a family run restaurant and provides fresh, wholesome, real food from regions of India, Bangladesh and Pakistan. With its vast array of dishes Sheba is curry heaven for meat, seafood and vegetarians alike.

Some of the Signature dishes include the delicious slow cooked lamb shank curry in a lovely bhuna sauce, the spicy grilled Lamb Chops, the authentic Bengal Roast Chicken Biryani, the sublime sweet and hot lamb with pumpkin, and the amazing Chittagong Fish Supreme.
